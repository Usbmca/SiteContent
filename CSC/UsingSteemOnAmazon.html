Cut from their tutorial.

  • Step 1: Go to Amazon and find the item that you would like to purchase.
  • Step 2: Add the item you would like to purchase to a wishlist or create one if you have not already got one. We have provided pictures below these steps to show you how.
  • Step 3: Get your wishlist link (Make sure it is set to public and your home address is selected)
  • Step 4: Send us your link on Discord - https://discord.gg/ux6Dm2G
  • Step 5: We will then give you a quote (15 - 20% more than Amazon prices)
  • Step 6: If you agree on the price send the total amount of SBD we tell you to and we will fulfill your order!
